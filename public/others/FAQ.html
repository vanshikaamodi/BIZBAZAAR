Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is BidBazaar?

BidBazaar is an online auction platform where users can bid on unique, rare, and antique items listed by verified sellers. The highest bidder at the end of the auction wins the product.

2. How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the top-right corner. Fill in the required details and verify your email to activate the account.

3. How do I place a bid?

1. Log in to your account
2. Browse available auctions
3. Click on a product
4. Enter your bid amount and click "Place Bid"
You'll be notified if you're outbid.

4. Can I cancel my bid?

No. Once placed, a bid cannot be canceled. Please bid carefully.

5. How will I know if I’ve won the auction?

You’ll receive an email notification if you're the highest bidder when the auction ends. The product will also be added to your cart automatically.

6. How can I list a product for auction?

Log in as a seller, go to the "Add Product" section, fill in the product details, set an auction duration, and upload images. After admin approval, your product will go live.

7. Are there any fees for using BidBazaar?

Registering and bidding are free. A small commission is charged to sellers upon successful auction sales.

8. What payment methods are accepted?

We support credit/debit cards, UPI, and PayPal. Payment instructions are shared once the auction ends.

9. Is my data safe on BidBazaar?

Yes. We use end-to-end encryption and follow strict security protocols to protect user data.

10. Who do I contact for support?

For any queries, email us at support@bidbazaar.com or use the contact form on the "Contact Us" page.
