Help

1. Getting Started

  • Learn how BidBazaar works
  • Register and create your account to start bidding or listing items.
  • Familiarize yourself with our bidding rules and guidelines.

2. Placing Bids

  • Find rare and antique items in ongoing auctions.
  • Set your maximum bid and let the system handle the rest.
  • Track your bids in real time through your account dashboard.

3. Selling Items

  • Create a listing for your antiques or rare items.
  • Add detailed descriptions, images, and a starting bid price.
  • Monitor and manage your auctions in your seller dashboard.

4. Payment and Shipping

  • Secure payment options for both buyers and sellers.
  • Understand our shipping policies and guidelines for smooth delivery.
  • Dispute resolution support in case of delivery or payment issues.

5. FAQs

Visit our FAQ page for quick answers to common questions, including account setup, bidding rules, seller guidelines, and payment security.
